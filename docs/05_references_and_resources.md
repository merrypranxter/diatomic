## Notes on image reuse

Always check the license before using images from external sources. Many SEM images in the literature are under standard copyright and require permission. Prefer:

- Images on Wikimedia Commons with CC or public domain licenses.
- Images from open-access papers where the license is stated explicitly.
- Your own renders (released under the repo's code license).

Document license and attribution in `images/README.md` and in the relevant catalog page.
